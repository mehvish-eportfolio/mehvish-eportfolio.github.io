Teacher Assistant Task


Course: Classroom Management
Topic: Classroom Seating Arrangements

For my teacher assistant task, I chose the topic of classroom seating arrangements. I chose this topic because classroom seating arrangements have a great impact on student learning, motivation, involvement, and teacher-student and student-student relationships. I focused on how teachers may boost student engagement by changing the physical design of chairs, tables, and presentations in the classroom. The classroom layout influences instructor pedagogy, activity selection, and on-task student behavior.

I decided to use Ahaslides as a mode of delivery for my lecture in class. Throughout my lesson, I focused on the influence and significance of classroom seating arrangements. I also had a list of questions prepared, such as What is the significance of classroom seating arrangements? What are the various factors that influence the seating arrangements in a classroom? To properly engage my colleague in the class I also showed a video about the advantages of flexible seating. I asked my colleagues a question at the end of the lesson, and they had to respond on Padlet.

I also made a Scoop It link with 12 various sources of knowledge on my topic, which was extremely helpful in planning my class.
