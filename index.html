About Me


Thank you for taking the time to look at my e-portfolio. Let me tell you a little about myself. I am a Pakistani national who relocated to the United Arab Emirates in 2007. My own country awarded me a master's degree in English Language Teaching and Linguistics and a bachelor's degree in English as a Second Language. It is not an exaggeration to state that a great teacher has the power to change a student's life, so this inspired me to become a teacher. I always wanted to be a great teacher. I've gained a plethora of information and talents that I believe would be best applied to educating and motivating the younger generation to achieve their desired targets and to make a huge difference in their lives. I feel that my PG diploma in teaching, as well as my unique ability to create excellent lesson plans and implement them effectively in the classroom, can make a significant difference to develop a knowledgeable society.
