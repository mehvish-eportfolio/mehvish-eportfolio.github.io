Lesson Plans


Every day, teachers utilize a lesson plan to determine what students will learn, how the lesson will be presented, and how learning will be assessed. I got the opportunity to prepare two lesson plans in my field of specialization during this course. My first lesson plan was for 5th-grade students to learn about different types of sentences, and my second lesson plan was for 3rd-grade children to learn about verbs.

As we all know, online teaching is difficult for both teachers and students. There were many different aspects that I as a teacher kept in my mind while preparing my lesson plans:

  • What kind of technology can I incorporate into my class to make it more engaging?
  • What are the objectives that I need to achieve at the end of the lesson?
  • The warmup activity must be related to the topic you're going to teach.
  • What kind of activities that I can use to accomplish the objectives of the lessons?
  • The content of the lesson and the usage of technology must be appropriate for the grade level.
  • How can I check studentsâ€™ understanding level at the end of lessons?
