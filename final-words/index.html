Final Words


This degree will bring a new dimension to my teaching abilities as a professional English language educator. This program covers courses that are essential for teaching subjects such as English, Science, and Mathematics, as well as courses that help students think critically and solve problems. This curriculum prepares teachers to assist students in making the most of their cognitive skills to achieve academic success. Collaborating with other members of the group has given me a lot of experience. All the professors in the ADU Education department are extremely knowledgeable and professional. I am now confident in my professional abilities because of their good coaching along the voyage. Overall, it was a pleasant experience.

I'd likex to express my gratitude to Dr. Reem Hashem for her unwavering support during this course. She taught me a lot of new ideas that would undoubtedly benefit me as a teacher in the future.

Mehvish Tanveer Winter 2021
