PG Diploma Reflection and Recommendations


This program places a strong emphasis on hands-on teaching experience. The program's curriculum is complemented by current research on effective teaching and learning approaches. This training helped me to better understand the demands of the teaching profession and broadened my practical knowledge. Although there are a few suggestions that might be useful:

  • The practical course will be more effective if the university includes real school instruction, such as clinical training, in addition to the course for students who are not currently employed.
  • If there is a guaranteed teaching job, it will be useful to students who are not currently employed, and more students will enroll in the program.
  • More activities can be introduced during online classes to boost student participation.
Mehvish Tanveer Winter 2021
